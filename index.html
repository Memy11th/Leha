    About us

    Leha Group, which originated in the Saudi desert, has expanded to include several companies serving diverse sectors.
    Our journey began in 1995 with the production of 2,000 tons of potatoes. It has continued to grow thanks to the trust our customers have given us. In 2003, we launched a fleet of refrigerated trucks to facilitate transportation. We also established Leha Refrigeration and Storage, a project that includes the largest cold storage warehouses in Saudi Arabia.
    Then Leha Trading and Marketing was established to provide the best agricultural services, and provide seeds and agricultural equipment. In 2004, we established Leha Architectural Contracting, to provide building and construction services.
    We at Lehaa Group always strive to provide the best services and products to our customers, relying on professionalism and quality.
